Because Growing Old Should Never Mean Being Alone

At our Elderly Care NGO in Nagpur, we believe that every senior deserves respect, care, and a life filled with peace and dignity. We exist to ensure that elders—especially those who are vulnerable, abandoned, or physically challenged—are cared for with compassion, medical responsibility, and human warmth.

Who We Are

A Trusted Senior Citizen Care NGO in Maharashtra

We are a charitable organization dedicated to elderly care and support for physically challenged individuals.

Our mission is simple yet powerful:

To serve elders as family — with empathy, integrity, and professional care.

We provide free and supported services to:

  • Senior citizens aged 75 years and above
  • Physically challenged individuals (no age limit)
  • Elders who lack financial or family support

Through structured care, medical attention, and emotional companionship, we help elders live their later years with comfort and confidence.

Our Elderly Care Services in Nagpur

Comprehensive, Compassionate & Dignified Care

We offer a wide range of senior citizen care services in Maharashtra, designed to meet physical, emotional, and medical needs.

Assisted Living Care

For elders who need daily medical supervision and personal assistance.

Independent Living

For seniors who are medically stable but seek community, safety, and support.

Home Healthcare Services

Professional nursing and caregiver support for elders who prefer care at home in Nagpur.

Dementia & Memory Care

Secure, structured, and emotionally sensitive care for seniors with dementia.

Post-Operative & Recovery Care

Support for elders recovering from surgery, injury, or hospitalization.

Chronic Illness Management

Care for arthritis, heart disease, asthma, cancer, and long-term conditions.
