A Charitable Trust Dedicated to
Elderly & Physically Challenged Care

We are a charitable trust focused on elderly care and support for physically challenged individuals in Nagpur. Rooted in compassion and guided by responsibility, our work is centered on one belief:

"Every human life deserves dignity, care, and respect—at every stage and in every ability."

Who We Serve

As an elderly care trust in Nagpur, we exist to serve senior citizens and physically challenged individuals who need medical support, emotional care, and a safe environment to live with peace and confidence.

Senior Citizens

Especially those aged 75 years and above who need dedicated support.

Physically Challenged

Universal assistance for individuals of all ages with varied abilities.

Vulnerable Elders

Supporting those who lack family support or financial stability.

"We work closely with caregivers, healthcare professionals, volunteers, and the local community to ensure that care is not just provided—but genuinely felt."

Our Mission

"Elder Joy Care Center is committed to providing a nurturing home where residents experience warmth, care, and a sense of belonging. Through personalized attention, we aim to enhance their well-being and bring joy to their lives."

Our Vision

"Elder Joy Care Center aims to be a warm and caring home, treating everyone like family. We aspire to create an environment where residents thrive, prioritizing their well-being and bringing daily joy."

Our Values

Dignity Above All

We respect every individual’s life journey and ensure that care is delivered with dignity, privacy, and empathy.

Compassion in Action

Care is not only medical—it is emotional, social, and human. We treat every resident as family.

Integrity & Transparency

As a senior citizen NGO in Maharashtra, we are committed to ethical operations, transparent processes, and responsible use of resources.

Community Responsibility

We believe elder care is a shared social responsibility, and we actively engage communities, volunteers, and partners in our mission.

The Heart of Our Approach

Our approach combines structured healthcare systems with deep emotional understanding, allowing elders to live with comfort rather than fear, and confidence rather than dependency.
