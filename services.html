Elderly Nursing Care in Nagpur

We provide professional, round-the-clock elderly nursing care delivered by trained and compassionate staff to ensure comfort, hygiene, and safety.

Nursing support includes:

  • Assistance with daily activities such as bathing, feeding, and mobility
  • Medication administration and monitoring
  • Post-hospitalization nursing care
  • Support for bedridden and mobility-impaired elders
  • Compassionate end-of-life care

All care is delivered patiently and respectfully, prioritizing dignity over routine.

Medical Support & Regular Health Check-Ups

Our structured medical care model focuses on early detection, continuity, and reduced hospital dependency.

Medical services include:

  • Regular doctor consultations
  • Monitoring of vital parameters (BP, sugar, O2)
  • Preventive health check-ups
  • Management of chronic conditions
  • Coordination with hospitals and specialists

This ensures consistent medical oversight and timely intervention.

Home Visits & Elderly Care at Home

For seniors who prefer to age in their own homes, we offer professional home care services for elderly in Nagpur.

Home-based support includes:

  • Nursing and caregiver visits
  • Health check-ups conducted at home
  • Medication reminders and assistance
  • Emotional support and regular monitoring
  • Guidance for family members and caregivers

This service allows elders to remain safe and supported within familiar surroundings.

Free vs Supported Elderly Care Services

Transparent & Ethical Structure

Free Care Services

  • Senior citizens aged 75 years and above
  • Physically challenged individuals (any age)
  • Includes nursing, medical, wellness & daily assistance

Supported Services

  • Senior citizens below 75 years
  • Specialized or extended medical care
  • Customized elderly care plans

All service structures are communicated clearly, ethically, and without hidden conditions.
