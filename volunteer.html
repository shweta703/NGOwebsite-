Volunteer With Us — Be the Reason an Elder Smiles

Volunteer for an Elderly Care NGO in Nagpur

At our Elderly Care NGO in Nagpur, volunteering is not about giving time—it is about sharing humanity. Many elders come to us seeking not just care, but connection, conversation, and compassion.

By choosing to volunteer, you help reduce loneliness, support physically challenged individuals, strengthen community responsibility, and create joyful moments through your presence.

Why Volunteering Matters

As people age, what they miss most is often not medicine—but companionship. By choosing to volunteer, you help:

Reduce loneliness and emotional isolation

Support physically challenged individuals

Strengthen community responsibility

Create joyful moments through your presence

Volunteering reminds our elders that they are seen, valued, and respected.

Volunteer Roles & Opportunities

Spend Time With Elders

  • Engage in conversations and companionship
  • Listen to their life stories and experiences
  • Share meals and celebrate special occasions
  • Play indoor games or read to them

Wellness & Activity Support

  • Organize gentle physical activities or yoga sessions
  • Lead music, art, or craft workshops
  • Assist in recreational outings and group activities
  • Help with gardening or light outdoor activities

Care & Support Assistance

  • Help with daily mobility and movement support
  • Assist during meal times
  • Support hygiene and personal care routines (with training)
  • Accompany elders to medical appointments

Administrative & Outreach Support

  • Help with documentation and record-keeping
  • Assist in social media and awareness campaigns
  • Support fundraising and donation drives
  • Coordinate volunteer schedules and events

CSR & Group Volunteering

  • Organize team-building activities with elders
  • Conduct health camps or awareness sessions
  • Sponsor special events (birthdays, festivals)
  • Provide skill-based volunteering (legal, medical, tech support)

What You Gain as a Volunteer

  • A sense of purpose and fulfillment
  • Real-world experience in social work and caregiving
  • Meaningful connections with elders and fellow volunteers
  • Personal growth through empathy and patience
  • Volunteer certificate and recognition
  • Opportunity to make a tangible difference in someone's life
